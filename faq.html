Frequently Asked Questions

What is the American Born Chinese Project?

The American Born Chinese Project is a creative submission that combines storytelling, visual art, and worldbuilding. It presents an original fantasy narrative along with supporting imagery and information to create a cohesive and immersive experience.

Who is the author of this project?

This project was created by Anant Parikh as a submission related to the American Born Chinese assignment.

What is the main story about?

The story follows Atlas le Seshan, a reincarnated individual who was once a software developer named Maximus on Earth. Transported to a world of magic, pantheons, and demons, Atlas seeks power and prestige but must confront his inner demons and values when challenged by Vasuki at the gates of the Abyssal Myriad Palace.

What are the different magic affinities in this world?

There are four major magic affinities: fire, water, earth, and wind. Each has three ranks of advancement. Fire consists of flame, blaze, and inferno. Water has tide, ice, and flow. Wind includes zephyr, gale, and tempest. Earth encompasses ground, root, and mountain. Combined affinities also exist for those with multiple affinities, such as storm, tremor, spark, lightning, soil, and wood.

What are the major pantheons mentioned in the story?

The significant pantheons are Ragnoros (Norse mythology), Olympia (Olympian Gods), Devanghariuos (Hindu gods), and Gladias (Roman mythology). Each pantheon has their own religion and religious branches within the world.

What is the Temporal Palace?

The Temporal Palace is an institute for noble scholars and knights, serving as an academy for the upper echelon and those who display immense potential. It is a place where all pantheons nurture intelligence and strength, and where Atlas spent his formative years before being called to the Abyssal Myriad Palace.

Who is Serpentarius?

Serpentarius is a forsaken god's first child who claims to be born of virtue, fallen to sin, yet standing in chaos. Serpentarius is the one who offers Atlas the chance to enter the Abyssal Myriad Palace and gain power rivaling gods and demons alike.

What is the Abyssal Myriad Palace?

The Abyssal Myriad Palace is a place where the most powerful techniques are contained. It is the resting place of Danu within the world and is guarded by Vasuki, the lord of waters. Entry requires passing a rite of passage and demonstrating virtue and proper values.

Who is Vasuki and what role does he play?

Vasuki is the lord of waters and naga of the demonic sea who guards the entrance to the Abyssal Myriad Palace. He tests those who wish to enter by evaluating their character, values, and virtue. Vasuki believes that true demons are those who wield diligence and patience, not malice and greed.

What are the grimoires mentioned in the gallery?

The grimoires are sacred magical texts preserved from ancient knowledge. They include Ars Almadel (celestial magic), Ars Paulina (angelic and planetary magic), Ars Notoria (magical knowledge and intellectual enhancement), Ars Theurgia (divine magic), and the Lesser Key of Solomon (seals of 72 demons). These texts contain knowledge of various magical practices and entities.

How can you stay true to yourself in the face of judgment from your peers?

In order to stay true to oneself in the face of peer pressure or judgement one must isolate themselves from the world. Isolate from all worldly factors and maintain a strict and firm resolve by wielding and honing a talent. My project is me embracing my love for coding, computer science, computer programming, anime, and manwha/manhua/manga.

Stereotypes exist primarily due to the first impressions between demographics. They are often inaccurate, yet sometimes they are true.
